Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

1931 - 2015

"You have to dream before your dreams can come true. A dream is not that which you see while sleeping, it is something that does not let you sleep."

The Missile Man of India

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and statesman who served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. Born in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, he studied physics and aerospace engineering. He spent the next four decades as a scientist and science administrator, mainly at the DRDO and ISRO.

Life Highlights

1931 Born in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu.
1960 Joined DRDO as a scientist.
1969 Transferred to ISRO; became Project Director of SLV-III.
1997 Awarded the Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian honor.
2002 Became the 11th President of India.
